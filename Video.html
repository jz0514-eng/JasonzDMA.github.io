Video

Work created with non-linear video editing softwares like Adobe Premiere Pro.
Road Trip

This artwork is a video depicting a story of going on a road trip to a forest. This is created within Adobe Premiere Pro by merging different segments of video with the theme of nature and sightseeing

Procrastination

This artwork is a video depict story of procrasting and start panicking when near the deadline of a projecy. This is created within Adobe Premiere Pro by merging different segments of video, and adding a clock sound effect that progressively get louder and faster to create a sense of urgency.

Inspiration

This artwork is a video depicting a story gathering idea by going outdoor for Inspiration. This is created within Adobe Premiere Pro by merging different segments of video and sound recording by experimenting with recording sound and video separately and recombining them with in Adobe Premiere Pro.

Dream

This artwork is a video depicting doing daily routine but strange thing start happening until the main character wake up . This is created within Adobe Premiere Pro by merging different segments of video and experimenting with different effect to create surreal dream like effect near the end.

Daily Routine

This artwork is a video with two different side, one depicting a story about going to school and doing works, the other staying home and playing games. This work is created in Adobe Premiere Pro by merging different segments of video and experimenting with Dual channeling
