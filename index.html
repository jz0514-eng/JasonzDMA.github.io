My name is Jason Zheng, and I am a student at San José State University, majoring in Digital Media Art with a focus on UI/UX design. Growing up heavily influenced by video games and visual media, I became interested in how digital artwork can express different emotions and messages that are not possible with just words.

At San José State University, I experimented with various media, including 3D modeling, Video art, Photography, and Coding, and I found myself drawn to photography and UI/UX design. I feel a unique sense of accomplishment when I create functional and visually engaging code in JavaScript, and I have found myself enjoying photography a lot, as I'm able to take more time to enjoy the view when I'm going out and see more things that I would have missed.

Currently, I am exploring ways to understand how to use UI/UX concepts more in my creative process. I am interested in how I could use JavaScript to bring my ideas to life.
