Procrastination - 2024

This artwork is a video depicting the story of procrastinating and starting to panic when near the deadline of a project. This is created within Adobe Premiere Pro by merging different segments of video and adding a clock sound effect that progressively gets louder and faster to create a sense of urgency.

This artwork holds a special meaning for me, as it is a reflection of my own life, where I always tell myself I still have time and I can do it later, until the deadly approach, and I start panicking.
